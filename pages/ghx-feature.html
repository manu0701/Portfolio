About my experience

As a product designer at Syngenta, I was deeply involved in designing various features for an app tailored to the needs of farmers. My work focused on creating intuitive, user-friendly solutions that simplified complex processes, ensuring farmers could easily access the tools and information they needed to enhance their operations. Through continuous user research and feedback, I iterated on designs that addressed real-world challenges faced by the farming community.

While I’m unable to showcase everything publicly due to confidentiality, I have a broader range of work and features that I’ve designed stored locally. These reflect my comprehensive involvement in shaping the product and refining its functionality to better serve its users.
